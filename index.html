About Our Brand

The Finest Brand started with me and my brothers. we were always into fashion and street wear. We wanted to stand out from the others and wear something different from everyone else, thats when we decided to start our own brand. we wanted something easy and simple, yet fashionable at the same time. we spent days coming up with a name for our brand. Our brand name came from one of our old photos and one my brothers called ourselves "Fine" thats when we came up with our brand nameFinest Clothing
